In Ethiopia, AI and software engineering jobs are growing fast in Addis Ababa, especially in companies like Ethio Telecom, banks, and startups.

Key skills in demand: Python, Machine Learning, Data Science, Cloud Computing, SQL.

For entry-level roles, having a strong GitHub portfolio is very important.

Cover letters should highlight relevant projects and enthusiasm for the company.

Interview tips: Prepare for technical questions on algorithms, Python, and problem-solving.
